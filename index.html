[Biography][^Top]

I am currently an associate professor at School of Automation, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Institute of Computing Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2009, under the supervision of Prof. Weiqing Tang. I got my BS degree and MS degree in computer science from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology in 2004 and 2006, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Huizhong Wu and Prof. Weiqing Li.

[Research Interests][^Top]

My research interests span Computer Graphics, Augmented Reality, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning. More specifically:

  • Point Cloud Processing: Denoising, Compression, Segmentation, Recognition, Quality Assessment.
  • Machine Learning for Graphics: Metric Learning, Hash Learning, Graph Learning, Representation Learning.
  • Rendering for AR: Shadow Rendering, Occlusion Handling, Relighting.
  • Modelling: 3D Reconstruction, Retrieval.
  • MoCap: Clustering, Retrieval.
  • Multimedia Security: Content Authentication for CAD, Image Authentication.
